Guidance for Collaborating with Volunteers & Technical Communities

Published August 2012

This document is intended for formal humanitarian entities that are willing to collaborate with CV & T. It is particularly suited to the needs of organizations previously exposed to CV & T and their work that seek practical advice in order to grow. This guide aims to enable stakeholders to better understand the dynamics of working with CV & T, better formulate their demands and maximize the benefits of such collaboration.

This document does not purport to resolve the ongoing debates on verification, reliability, added value of crowdsourcing, data privacy and other issues currently being addressed in other forums.

We express our gratitude to the actors in the humanitarian community and the CV & T who shared their ideas, knowledge and experiences in writing this document.
